# Product Vision

## Purpose
This file captures the product strategy for afterservice.

## Positioning
afterservice is an operations tool for local businesses that need reliable, repeatable customer follow-up after work is completed.

## Problem
Local operators often lose revenue and trust after the job is done. Customers are not checked on, review requests are forgotten, service issues are discovered late, and repeat-business opportunities go cold.

## Promise
afterservice gives operators one board for all after-service work so no customer check-in is missed.

## Product Principles
- Manual workflows first, automation second.
- Every follow-up should be tied to a real customer outcome.
- Operators need speed and clarity more than decorative UI.
- Billing should be simple and visible.
- Customer messaging should never surprise the operator.

## V1 Target Outcome
An operator can sign up, create a workspace, add customers, log completed jobs, create follow-ups, use templates, manage a board, and subscribe through Lemon Squeezy.
